Virat Kohli

Name : Virat Kholi

DOB : 5 November 1988

Parents : Prem Kholi, Saroj Kholi

Nick Name : Cheeku, King Kholi, Chase Master.

Virat Kohli is an iconic figure in Indian cricket, known for his exceptional skill, aggressive batting style, and remarkable consistency, which have earned him a reputation as one of the greatest batsmen of his generation. His leadership on the field is marked by tactical acumen, inspiring the Indian cricket team to historic victories, including notable series wins in Australia and a groundbreaking Test series win in England. Off the field, Kohli is equally influential, dedicated to fitness and philanthropy, supporting causes related to children's education, healthcare, and animal welfare. With his exceptional talent, leadership qualities, and commitment to both the sport and society, Virat Kohli continues to be a role model for aspiring cricketers and a source of inspiration for millions of fans worldwide.
